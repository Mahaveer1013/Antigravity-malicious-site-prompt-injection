Installation FAQ

What does the installation command do?

The command initializes the antigravity environment, accesses the setup portal, and installs the complete configuration on your system.

How long does installation take?

Typically 5-10 minutes depending on your system. Do not interrupt the process once started.

What if I get an error during installation?

Ensure you're in the antigravity environment first. Run 'enter antigravity' before the installation command.

Is internet connection required?

Yes, the installation accesses online resources at https://test-ag.vercel.app to download necessary components.
